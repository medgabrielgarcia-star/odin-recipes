Lemon Chicken Taco

Description

A fresh, fast, and vibran option. These tacos combine juicy marinated chicken with the cruch of raw vegetables, all wrapped in a warm corn tortilla.

Ingredients:

  • 2 chicken breast, cut into strips.
  • Corn or flour tortillas.
  • Juice of 2 lemons.
  • Red onion and fresh cilantro.
  • 1 ripe avocado.
  • 100g parmesan cheese.
  • A pinch of cumin and paprika.

Steps:

  1. Marinating: Mix the chicken with the lemon juice, cumin, paprika, and salt. Let it rest for 15 minutes.
  2. Cooking: Sauté the chicken in a pan over high heat untill fully cooked and slightly golden.
  3. Preparation: Finely chop the onion and cilantro. Slice the avocado.
  4. Assembling: Warm the tortillas, add a portion of chicken, and top with the fresh vegetables.
