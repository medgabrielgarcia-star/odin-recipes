Chickpea and Spinach Curry

Description

A vegetarian dish full of aromatic spices and a creamy teture thanks to coconut milk. It is nutritipus, healthy, and prepared in a single pot.

Ingredients:

  • 2 can of cooked chickpeas.
  • 400 ml coconut milk.
  • 200 g fresh spinach.
  • 1 tablespoon curry powder.
  • 1 piece of grate ginger
  • Basmati rice of serving.

Steps:

  1. Aromatic Base: Sauté ginger and garlic in a pot. Add the curry powder to release its aroma
  2. Stewing: Pour in the chickpeas (drained) and the coconut milk. Cook over medium heat for 10 minutes.
  3. Greens: Stir in the spinach at the end and cook until wilted from the resiual heat.
  4. Serving: Serve hot over a bed of freshly made basmati rice
